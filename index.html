About the Game

UPRO is a fast-paced action-adventure game where players explore a surreal world, solve environmental puzzles, and face uniquely themed enemies.

Gameplay: Navigate branching levels, upgrade abilities, and master intuitive controls. Features include local co-op, customizable loadouts, and a dynamic soundtrack that reacts to your playstyle.
